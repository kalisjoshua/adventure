# Prosper (a game)

1. Chop wood and cook food
   - Surplus can be a bad thing
   - Producing too much makes production go down
2. Build shelter
3. Organize a population
   - Assign people to jobs: food, wood, research
4. Research
   - Mind | Intellectual
   - Body | Physical
   - Soul | Spiritual
5. Disasters
   - Thieves
   - Famine
   - Spoilage
   - Rot
6. Currency
   - Reputation
   - Influence - Force
7. Explore
